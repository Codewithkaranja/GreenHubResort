About Us

GreenHub Resort Masii is a stylish hotel in Machakos County, just 1 minute from Masii Town and Kitui Road. We offer modern rooms, elegant interiors, and scenic views of the Eastern Kenya hills — perfect for both business and leisure stays.

Enjoy top amenities including a sparkling pool, on-site restaurant, and fully equipped conference hall. Whether you're here for a quick getaway or a corporate event, GreenHub Resort blends comfort, convenience, and great hospitality.
