About Us


Welcome to GreenHub Resort Masii – your premier destination for comfort, style,
and unforgettable moments in the heart of Machakos County. Designed with elegance and class,
our resort features beautifully furnished interiors, spacious modern rooms,
and a dedicated professional staff committed to delivering a top-tier experience.

Ideally located just a 1-minute drive from Masii Town and Kitui Road,
GreenHub Resort offers stunning panoramic views of the surroundin mountain ranges and
the breathtaking landscapes of the Eastern Region. Whether you're here for business or leisure,
we have everything you need under one roof. Take a refreshing swim in our sparkling swimming pool,
savor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant, or host your events and meetings in our fully equipped
conference hall. From seamless hospitality to serene surroundings,GreenHub Resort Masii is where luxury
meets convenience. Come stay with us and experience the difference.
